# Learning anything with Zoonk

<PostMeta date="May 26, 2026" authorId="will" />

We launched the first version of Zoonk.

The idea is simple: tell us what you want to learn, and we build a course for you.

<YouTube id="2DI_fSkLnh4" />

## How it works

You type a topic or goal, like "quantum physics" or "Spanish."

Zoonk turns that into course ideas, then creates a course with chapters and lessons. You do not need to know how to plan the course yourself. The app breaks the subject into smaller pieces so you can just start learning.

## Lessons are interactive

The lessons are not just pages of text.

Zoonk can explain hard ideas in everyday language, use examples you already understand, and show things with charts, diagrams, timelines, code, formulas, tables, images, music, and quotes when they help.

You also practice. Some lessons ask questions, give feedback, or make you choose what to do in a situation. If you get something wrong, the point is not just "wrong." The lesson should help you understand why.

For language learning, Zoonk can also create vocabulary, grammar, translation, listening, reading, and writing-system lessons when they fit the course.

## Progress should feel useful

Zoonk tracks your score so you can see your accuracy over time.

It also shows patterns like your best day and best time to learn. That matters because learning is easier to improve when you can see what is working. If you usually perform better on Tuesday mornings, that is useful to know.

## Levels, not just completion

As you learn, your Brain Power goes up. It never goes down, because knowledge is not something you lose because you missed a day.

Brain Power moves you through belt levels, from white belt up to black belt. It gives your progress a simple shape: keep learning, keep leveling up.

## Energy instead of streaks

We also added energy.

Correct answers increase it. Wrong answers and missed days can lower it. But it is not a streak that breaks and makes you feel like you failed if you missed a day.

Life happens. So, when you miss a day, your energy dips instead of losing your entire streak. Then it can recover when you come back.
